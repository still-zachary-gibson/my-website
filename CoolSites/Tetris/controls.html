Player 1/Right Handed:
⇄: Move piece left or right, if possible.
↓: Soft drop by one row. Worth 1 point.
↑: Hard drop, worth 2 points per row.
Player 2/Right Handed:
Z: Rotate counter clockwise.
X: Rotate clockwise.
C: Hold piece, replacing your piece with the held piece or the next one, and puting it in the held slot. You can't unhold while the new piece is falling!
Shift: Pause/Unpause game.
Enter: Start or end game.

Player 2/Left Handed:
AD: Move left or right.
S: Soft drop.
W: Hard drop.
Player 1/Left Handed:
,: Rotate counter-clockwise.
.: Rotate clockwise.
/: Hold piece
Shift: Pause/Unpause game.
Enter: Start or end game.
