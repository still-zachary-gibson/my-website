This is one heck of a thing, so hold on folks!

TILE EDITOR:
Click: Places or replaces the selected tile.
⇄/AD: Change tile to place down, including spikes and empty space.
⇅/WS: Change tileset across whole level.
Shift: Drag to replace multiple tiles in one click.
S: Show tile code.Space: Toggle tile guide.
Enter: Export PNG of level.
P: Enter playtesting mode.
F:Enter collision editer.

COLLISION EDITOR:
Click: Create or select a collision block.
↔↕: Move block.
WASD: Change height and width of block.
Backspace: Remove block.
P: Show block code.
F:Return to tile editor.
Q: Enter spike editor.
Click (Spike): Add or remove spike at tile.

PLAY TESTING:
WASD and Arrows: Move character.
Space: Jump.
P: Return to tile editor.

Misc:
Escape: Clear code at the top.
Enter a tile code into the first bar, then click load to load it in. Enter a name into the second bar to save your level when pressing ALT as it.
The two X and Y things are respectively for your playtesting spawn location, and for the width and height of the canvas, both in tiles.
