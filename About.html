About Me

This is the page where I kinda just talk about myself a bit, so I can't really blame you if you'd much rather
not bother to read this page. Frankly, probably a better use of your time, but I suppose if you really
want to, them this is the page to go to!

So, who am I? Not sure why you're asking me as if I know, but my name's Zachary Gibson.
Currently a Junior, but about to be a Senior. I'm a big fan of computers, mostly stuff like coding and
software to pick the stuff I'm most into. I also enjoy reading, swimming, and have a dog who's
only kind of a massive jerk most of the time. I kid, he's great and I love him a lot.

I'm the type of person who's basically always working, except for when my case of
executive disfunction says no to me, and I kinda just sadly slink away.
But that's besides the point, I like doing things, plain and simple.
Usually stuff relating to programming and/or game development, but really anything that
occupies time works, because if I don't have things I'm doing...
My brain's not really a fan of that, and that's not a fun time.

I also enjoy playing games of quite a few kinds, though my tastes can be
a bit all over the place really. I've also worked on a few games, such as the
ones you can find in the JS Gallery, and as part of a team on multiple
projects that are either dead, not having much go on, or other states of general
"probably not going to be finished"ness, due to how teamwork is hard.
But that's just the way things go!

I've also been in a few competitions, such as the SkillsUSA one,
where I didn't place dead last, so that's certainly something.

I might not be the best at peopling, but I try my best to be nice, and to
help people out whenever I can. Even if sometimes I suck at doing
both of those things, but I try my best.

I have no clue what else to put here, despite the fact there's probably loads more
to mention, but I say we can call it here. Bye!
